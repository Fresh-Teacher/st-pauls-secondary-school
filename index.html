F.A.Q

These are some of the frequently asked questions about our school and their answers.

  • Do you only enroll Catholic Students?

    No! We accept all students from different beliefs.

  • Does your school follow the national curriculum.

    Yes, our school teaches following the syllabus and curriculum as provided by the National Development Curriculum Centre and the Ministry Of Education and Sports.

  • Do you have bursaries at your school?

    Yes! We provide half and full bursaries to different categories of students like the talented ones and the disadvantaged ones.

  • Can I register my child now?

    Yes! Registration is in progress at our school. Please reach us on the contacts provided to register your child today.

  • Can I pay the school fees in installments?

    Yes! You can first clear all the school requirements and then pay the school fees in installments till it is fully cleared.
